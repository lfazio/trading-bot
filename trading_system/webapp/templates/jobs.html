Strategies

Reference for the strategies you can pick in the onboarding wizard or backtest run. Both follow the project's after-tax-optimisation + tax-aware-trade-gate discipline.

CoreStrategy

Long-term holding, dividend compounding, low turnover. Targets the phase's STOCK allocation bucket and proposes BUYs from the top of the screener ranking when current exposure falls behind the target by at least the rebalance band. Existing holdings are skipped — the strategy never proposes SELLs (liquidation is the risk engine's stop-loss job or the operator's).

Best for the early phases (Capital Builder, Stability) where fee minimisation matters. Survival > return.

TacticalStrategy

Trend / breakout / pullback signal generator. For each top-N candidate from the screener ranking, evaluates three signals on the recent daily bars:

  • Trend — short MA strictly above long MA (e.g. 20d > 50d).
  • Breakout — today's close strictly above the prior N-day high.
  • Pullback — in an uptrend, today's close at or below the long MA ("buy the dip").

Any signal triggers a BUY proposal with a tight stop-loss; the per-trade size comes from the lower bound of the phase's risk-per-trade band so tactical sizing stays phase-consistent.

Best for systematic + capital-acceleration phases. Higher turnover; fees scale with activity.
